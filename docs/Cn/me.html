Matrix Element Formulas

The formula provided in Theorem 1.1 of

  • Molev, A. I. "A basis for representations of symplectic Lie algebras." Communications in mathematical physics 201 (1999): 591-618

gave the actions of the generators \(F_{i,j} = E_{i,j} - \left(\mathrm{sgn}i\cdot \mathrm{sgn}j\right) E_{-j,-i}\). The matrices are indexed by \(-n, -n+1, \ldots, -1,1,\ldots,n\).

\[ F_{kk}\zeta_{\Lambda}=\left(2\sum_{i=1}^{k}\lambda_{ki}^{\prime}-\sum_{i=1}^{k}\lambda_{ki}-\sum_{i=1}^{k-1}\lambda_{k-1,i}\right)\zeta_{\Lambda} \] \[ F_{k,-k}\zeta_{\Lambda}=\sum_{i=1}^{k}A_{ki}(\Lambda)\zeta_{\Lambda+\delta_{ki}^{\prime}} \] \[ F_{-k,k}\zeta_{\Lambda}=\sum_{i=1}^{k}B_{ki}(\Lambda)\zeta_{\Lambda-\delta_{ki}^{\prime}} \] \[ F_{k-1,-k}\zeta_{\Lambda}=\sum_{i=1}^{k-1}C_{ki}(\Lambda)\zeta_{\Lambda-\delta_{k-1,i}}+\sum_{i=1}^{k}\sum_{j,m=1}^{k-1}D_{kijm}(\Lambda)\zeta_{\Lambda+\delta_{ki}^{\prime}+\delta_{k-1,j}+\delta_{k-1,m}^{\prime}} \] with the coefficients \[ A_{ki}(\Lambda)=\prod_{a=1,a\neq i}^{k}\frac{1}{l_{ka}^{\prime}-l_{ki}^{\prime}} \] \[ B_{ki}(\Lambda)=4A_{ki}(\Lambda)l_{ki}^{\prime}\prod_{a=1}^{k}\left(l_{ka}-l_{ki}^{\prime}\right)\prod_{a=1}^{k-1}\left(l_{k-1,a}-l_{k,i}^{\prime}\right) \] \[ C_{ki}(\Lambda)=\frac{1}{2l_{k-1,i}}\prod_{a=1,a\neq i}^{k-1}\frac{1}{l_{k-1,i}^{2}-l_{k-1,a}^{2}} \] \[ \begin{array}{ll} D_{kijm}(\Lambda)& =A_{ki}(\Lambda)A_{k-1,m}(\Lambda)C_{kj}(\Lambda)\times \\ &\prod_{a=1,a\neq i}^{k}\left(l_{k-1,j}-l_{ka}^{\prime}\right)\left(l_{k-1,j}+l_{ka}^{\prime}+1\right)\prod_{a=1,a\neq m}^{k-1}\left(l_{k-1,j}-l_{k-1,a}^{\prime}\right)\left(l_{k-1,j}+l_{k-1,a}^{\prime}+1\right) \end{array} \]
